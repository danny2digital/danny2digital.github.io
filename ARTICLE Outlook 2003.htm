How to Setup Office 2007, 2003 and 2000 for a good old email client and UI

Outlook clients depend on POP3 or IMAP in order to receive and send out emails using the client. Whether your email provider is gmail, yahoo, AOL, Juno, Outlook/Hotmail, you will have specific settings to incorporate first before you sign in with your email address on these clients.

For Gmail, accounts use IMAP so you will first add your account from scratch

1) enter your name (anything really)
2) enter the gmail address associated with your account
3) account type should be set as IMAP
4) Incoming mail server should be set as: imap.gmail.com
5) Outgoing mail server (SMTP) should be set as: smtp.gmail.com

Logon information
Username: your email address
password: your 2fa one time app password you can check on your gmail account

Do not click require SPA for logon, go to more settings:
- under outgoing server checkmark "my outgoing SMTP requires authentication and checkmark "use same settings as my incoming mail server"
- under connection checkmark connect using my local area network LAN
- under advanced incoming server (IMAP): 993 and SSL for encryption
- under advanced outgoing server (SMTP): 587 and TLS for encryption

You're now good to go for Gmail.
